### What are various operators supported by JavaScript?

JavaScript supports several types of operators, including arithmetic operators, comparison operators, logical operators, and assignment operators.

Examples:

- Arithmetic: `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`
- Comparison: `==`, `!=`, `>`, `<`
- Logical: `&&`, `||`
- Assignment: `=`, `+=`, `-=`
